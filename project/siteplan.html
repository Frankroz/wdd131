Site plan

Purpose

This website will be a digital hub for retro gaming enthusiasts. It will feature:

  • Console Spotlights: Articles exploring the history, design, and impact of iconic gaming consoles like the Nintendo 64, PlayStation, and Game Boy.

  • News and Events: Information on upcoming retro gaming events, conventions, and news related to the preservation of classic games.

  • Game Reviews: In-depth reviews of classic games across various platforms (NES, SNES, Sega Genesis, etc.), focusing on gameplay, graphics, music, and historical significance.

I have been a passionate retro gamer since childhood. I grew up playing classic games and have a deep appreciation for the artistry, innovation, and cultural impact of this era of gaming. I believe this project will allow me to share my passion with others and contribute to the preservation and celebration of gaming history.

Target audience

Nostalgic Gamers who grew up playing classic games and are seeking to reconnect with those experiences. Gaming Enthusiasts who appreciate the history and evolution of video games and are curious about the cultural impact of retro gaming.

Scenarios

How can I get involved in the retro gaming community? - Looking for information on local meetups, online forums, or clubs.

What are some of the most popular or sought-after retro games? - Looking for recommendations and insights from experts.

What are the benefits of playing retro games? - Exploring the educational, social, and cultural value of classic gaming.
